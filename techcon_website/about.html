History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for technology enthusiasts. over the years, it has evolved into one of the largest tech conferences, attracting professionals from around the world.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation, inspire collaboration and provide platform for the brightest minds in technology to share their ideas and insights. We aim to bridge the gap between visionaries and practitioners, empowering a brighter tech-driven future.
